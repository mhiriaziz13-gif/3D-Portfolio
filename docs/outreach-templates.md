# Ethical outreach templates

## Permission to reference a project

Subject: Permission to publish a public-safe project summary

I have prepared a concise case study describing my contribution to [project]. It excludes confidential data, customer details and commercial figures. Would you be willing to review the wording and confirm what may be published? No endorsement will be implied.

## Useful community contribution

Subject: Proposed contribution on [specific problem]

I can contribute a practical, evidence-led piece on [question], based on a public-safe workflow I worked on. The proposed outline is [outline]. It would include [diagram/checklist], cite authoritative sources and avoid promotional or confidential claims. Would this be useful to your audience?
